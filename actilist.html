What Is ActiList?

ActiList is an app that connects people with similar interests and helps users get better recommendations on topics like movies and music from people who they know personally and share similar taste.

Users can create lists of their interests and add their activities into these lists. Users can follow a person or just a particular list of that person.

With ActiList people don’t have to worry about fidning someone to discuss their unique taste in movies or books.

Create Lists of your interests

Books, Movies, Music, Travel. Create a list of anything that you care about and start adding activities into the lists as you do them.

Stay in touch

See what people like you are doing in the interest space. Learn about new activities that you know you will enjoy. You don’t have to regret watching that indie movie because it was rated highly by your friend.

Share your activities. Choose who can see.

Keep track of the interesting things you do.

You decide who gets to see the content. You can make private list / activity if you want to keep a personal list or share it with your followers.

Discover new interests

Search for activities or topics of your interest and discover activities by people on that topic. This is a great way to find people who like doing things you like to do.

Follow interesting people or lists

Follow interesting people and discover things they have posted. If you are only interested in a particular list of a person, follow only that list. Rest of the content from that person will not be shown in your feed.

Background

I have always enjoyed watching movies. In 2013 I started an project. I started making a spreadsheet list of all the movies I have watched so that I coudl get back to the list in the future and re-watch movies I liked.

My main source of discovering new movies has been IMDB. Sometimes I would watch a great movie and other times I felt like I wasted good 2 hours of my life even though a movie had great ratings. When that started happening more often and I started getting less time to spare, I turned towards few friends who recommendation I could always believe when it came to movie recommendations.

I then started wondering if both these ideas had anything in common. That’s when I felt the need of an app that let me find recommendation of topics I am interested in, be it movies or music or books, from people who I share the same taste as I did and not depend on arbitrary reviews posted in the internet.

Research

To validate the idea, I ran a survey on online recommendations and analysed data from 41 participants. Below are some important findings that helped me understand the users and problem better.

“I know my preferences and so do most of my friends so I trust their recommendations”

“Trust is the likely factor; friends or those close to me likely have same/similar tastes and preferences”

Wireframes

I began by skecthing and creating low-fidelity wireframes to define the interaction paths, navigational patterns and to test the concept with users. The wireframes went through a couple of rounds of iterations before they paved way for high-fidelity mock-ups

I ran a set of quick usability tests with people around me and some of the opportunities of improvement I learned are:

  • Ability to subscribe to lists created by people apart from follwoing the people.
  • Apply multiple interest filters instead of one.
  • Privacy options for individual activity posts.

Visual Design

I started the visual design process by creating a style guide to help me with the design process. Once I had my styles and patterns ready, I designed the screens and the improved the interactions based on the above feedback.

High Fidelity Mockups

Once sure about the right user flow, interaction patterns and the visual guide for the app, I designed the high-fidelity mockups. The feedback from the usability testing were also incorporated.

Benefits

AltiList helps users stay better connected to their interests and also with people with similar interests.

AltiList’s user-curated lists helps users can discover new and interesting activities to do and also connect with interesting people.

ActiList provides a faster and efficient way for users to gather opinion about a topic from people they know they can depend upon.

Reflections

This was my third detailed mobile interaction design project and I was quite pleased with the results I was able to achieve.

By keeping ‘Movies’ as my central theme, I was able to scope the project well and stay better focused on the story I was trying to design for.

I put in a lot more effort in the visual department because my previous work hadn’t met my expectations. I was able to create a more detailed style guide and build the visual elements based off that.

I decided to design for the iOS because I had never designed an iPhone app before and I got a chance to explore and work on iOS’ design principles.

Beyond

In this exercise I designed around a movie enthusiast’s experince. However, there were a bunch of ideas I would like to explore. Some of them are:

The app has a lot of potential to expand into other spaces of interests and even commerce. For example a restaurant might make a list of their menu which its patrons can subscribe to.

A clothes store can create lists of their collection and people can subscribe to a collection list they’re interested in.

In the future iterations I would like to explore content designs for commercial users and push the idea beyond the personal interest space.
